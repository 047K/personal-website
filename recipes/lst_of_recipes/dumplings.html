Dumplings

Dumpling recipe derived from Magic Ingredients and Chopstick chronicales

Ingredients

  • 60 Dumpling wrappers
  • 500g Beef, pork or chicken mince
  • Wombok 5 leaves
  • Soy sauce 16g
  • Ginger 5g
  • Garlic 5g
  • Seasume oil 5g
  • Egg white 1
  • Green onion 3
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • Oil 10g

Steps

  1. Finely cube the wombok. Set into a bowl and generously salt. Mix to combine and wait 10 minutes before squeezing the water out.
  2. Peel and finely chop the ginger and garlic. Finely chop the green onion
  3. To a bowl, add the mince, soy sauce, ginger, garlic, seasume oil, salt, pepper and green oil
  4. To a pan, heat up oil before adding to the mince mixture
  5. Add the wombok to the bowl and mix all together
  6. Using the dumpling wrappers, wrap the mince mixture
