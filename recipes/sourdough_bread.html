Sourdough bread

Sourdough bread recipe derived from Alexandra cooks

Ingredients

  • Sourdough starter 100g
  • Water 375g
  • Wheat flour 75g
  • Bread flour 425g
  • Salt 12g

Steps

  1. Whisk the starter, salt water together then add the flours
  2. Sit for 30 minutes then perform a 4 sets of stretch and folds
  3. Let bulk ferment according to chart
  4. Shape into a proofing basket and proof in the refrigerater overnight
  5. With a dutch oven in your oven, preheat the ovean to 290 celsius for 1 hour
  6. Transfer the loaf out of the proofing basket into parchment paper and set into the dutch oven
  7. Bake for 30 minutes with the lid and 20 minutes without the lid
